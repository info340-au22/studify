Introduction

We are group BA11 in INFO 340, the members in this group are Minh Mai and Luong Quan.

We are planning to make a study management speciacally a table study for student can set up and custom their own schedule.

How can you use it?

First, the user will need to created their own account for future use.

Then, we will ask them a short survey with annimation (about 10 questions) to create their own user interface base on their response.

Second, they can custom their own schedule for the week, including adding what time on specific day their homework going to be due, or when their quiz and exam will happen

One special thing that our web app will make user experience more better is that they can create their own study group with their peers by simply using the same platform and adding their peer name into the study room.
